Site Name: Connect with Nature

This name was selected because it encapsulates the essence of the website, which is to provide a serene digital oasis where visitors can immerse themselves in the beauty of the natural world.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to encourage users to create and share short stories, poems, and wildlife encounters. It aims to be a hub for nature enthusiasts to connect, share experiences, and find inspiration in the natural world.

Scenarios

  • How can I share my nature story or poem?
  • Where can I view the art gallery of nature images?
